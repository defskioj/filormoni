Our Heritage

Nine Decades of
Living Tradition

Founded in 1934 amid the vast steppes of Kazakhstan, the National Qazaq Philharmonic has served as the guardian of a musical heritage stretching back across the centuries. We unite the timeless voice of the Dombra with the grandeur of the Western symphonic tradition.

Each performance is an act of cultural memory — a bridge between the world of the Zhyrau bards and the concert halls of the modern world.
