The Artists

Our Ensemble

120 musicians united by one vision — where the ancient voice of the steppe meets the grandeur of the symphony.

Chief Conductor

Ayan Sultanov

Music Director · Since 2018

Ayan Sultanov studied at the Moscow Conservatory and Vienna before leading the Qazaq Philharmonic in 2018. His interpretations are celebrated for their emotional depth and cultural authenticity.

Under his leadership the orchestra has performed at Carnegie Hall, Berliner Philharmonie, and Suntory Hall in Tokyo.

Principal Soloists

The voices of our orchestra

Aigerim Bekova

Concertmaster · Violin

Winner of the International Violin Competition in Brussels. Aigerim leads the string section with unparalleled elegance since 2015.

Daulet Seitkali

Principal · Dombra

Virtuoso of the Dombra who pioneered its integration into the symphony orchestra, bridging two great musical worlds.

Asel Nurlanovna

Principal · Cello

Trained at the Paris Conservatoire, Asel anchors the cello section. Her warm, resonant tone has become a defining sound of our orchestra.
