A Century in Music

Key milestones

1934

Foundation in Almaty

The National Qazaq Philharmonic is established, becoming the first professional symphonic institution in Kazakhstan, bringing together the traditions of the steppe with the European orchestra.

1955

The Dombra Concerto Era

Legendary composer Akhmet Zhubanov premieres the first Dombra Concerto with a full symphony orchestra — a defining moment in Qazaq musical identity.

1991

Independence & Renewal

With Kazakh independence, the Philharmonic becomes a symbol of national renaissance — expanding its repertoire and embarking on its first international tours.

2024

90th Anniversary Season

A landmark year celebrating nine decades of excellence — world premieres, international collaborations and a renewed commitment to Qazaq musical heritage.

Pillars of Our Legacy

Visionaries who shaped the tradition

Akhmet Zhubanov

Composer & Founder · 1906–1968

The architect of Qazaq symphonic music. Zhubanov pioneered the synthesis of steppe melodies with Western classical forms, composing the first national opera and orchestral works.

Gaziza Zhubanova

Composer & Conductor · 1927–1993

Kazakhstan's first female composer and conductor of international renown. Her works — from chamber pieces to grand symphonies — brought the Qazaq soul to concert halls worldwide.

Nurgisa Tlendiyev

Conductor & Composer · 1925–1998

Master of the folk orchestra and beloved conductor. Tlendiyev preserved and elevated traditional Qazaq music, creating timeless arrangements still performed today across the world.

The Living Thread

The Steppe Voice
in Modern Music

The music of the Kazakh steppe is not a relic of the past — it is a living, breathing force that continues to shape contemporary composition. From the mournful cry of the Kobyz to the rhythmic pulse of the Dombra, these ancient voices speak in every note our orchestra plays.

Our musicians undergo rigorous training in both Western conservatoire traditions and Qazaq folk music — a duality that makes our sound unique in the world of classical music.
